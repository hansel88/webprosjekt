The royal family castle

The royal family castle is in the end of Oslo´s most famous street, Karl Johan´s street. The castle itself is one of the main buildings and a concrete symbol of Norwegian history since year 1814.

It was built in the first half of the 19th century, in residence of our King Charles 3, who also ruled as a king of Sweden and otherwise lived there. We had now a huge palace that was available to our king, who never lived there.
