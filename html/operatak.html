The Opera house in Oslo

The opera house in Oslo is the Norwegian opera and ballet house in Bjørvika at the head of Oslofjorden. Architectural firm Snøhetta designed the house. Thanks to this firm, who made it possible for you to run or walk on the top of the opera house. Where we can se the view pointing inwards our capital OSLO.

Nearest public transport. - Oslo Central Station.
